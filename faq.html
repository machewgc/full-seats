About Precinct Committepeople

How many Precinct Captains can be in a precinct?

Each precinct has two Precinct Captains, one Committeeman and one Committewoman.

What does a Precinct Captain do?

A Committee Chair talks to their neighbors! As a Committee Chair you will be responsible for engaging people in your precinct to find out what they feel the party platform should be, and then deliver that message on to the Democratic Party. Precinct Captains are a mouthpiece for voters to engage the party with.

I heard this called a different name?

Precinct Captains go by many names, and you may hear someone refer to them by a variety of other names.
  • Precinct Committeewoman\Committeeman
  • Precinct Chairwoman\Chairman
  • Precinct Committee Officer
  • Precinct Captain
  • Precinct Delegate
How to apply

Do I need approval from the Democratic Party?

No. Precinct Captain is an elected position, and you are free to submit your declaration of candidacy and then appear on the ballot where people in your precinct will vote.

Where can I apply?

On the maps page, you willl find a download link for the Candidacy Form. You will need to fill out this form, then have it notarized and mailed to your county election office.

How do I get my form notarized?

There are many Notary Publics available in Kansas and you can search for them here. Here are some places you can often find notary services.
  • Your local bank
  • City and town clerksâ€™ offices
  • Courts
  • Real estate, insurance or law offices
  • Travel Agencies
  • Your local drug store or pharmacy
  • FedEx Kinkos
  • UPS Store
You do not have to be a client of the bank or business to use a notary public. Notaries are public servants. Some notaries may charge a fee for service if you are not an existing client of theirs.

Do I have to pay to play?

No. There is no fee for subitting your declaration of candidacy for Precinct Captain.

Where do I send my declaration of Candidacy?

Your declaration of candidacy must be mailed to your county election office. On the map page you can put your address in to find your county election office address.

When do I get paid?

Our standard payment plan provides for monthly payments. At the end of each month, all accumulated funds are transfered to your account. The minimum amount of your balance should be at least 70 USD.
